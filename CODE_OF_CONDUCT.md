# Code of conduct

Bellwire is committed to a respectful, technically rigorous, and welcoming
community.

Participants must:

- communicate constructively and focus criticism on ideas and code;
- respect different backgrounds, identities, experience levels, and viewpoints;
- avoid harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized language, and unwanted
  personal attention;
- protect private information and never pressure others to disclose credentials
  or personal data;
- accept reasonable moderation decisions and correction in good faith.

These expectations apply in repository discussions, reviews, community spaces,
and public interactions where someone represents Bellwire.

Report conduct concerns privately to `feedback@bellwire.app` with `[CONDUCT]`
in the subject. Maintainers may edit or remove content, close discussions, or
temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary. Reports will
be handled as confidentially as practical, subject to safety and legal duties.

This policy is informed by the Contributor Covenant and may be replaced by the
full standard text before the first stable community release.
